# Aftly

*Find out exactly why you didn't get the job.*

Aftly listens to both sides of your interview call and gives you a scored breakdown of what you said, what you missed, and what to do differently next time.

---

## The Problem

### 1. You Don't Know What You Did Wrong
Most candidates finish interviews thinking they nailed it, only to receive a rejection email days later. Without feedback, you're left guessing what went wrong and how to improve.

### 2. Interview Feedback is Rare
Interviewers rarely provide detailed feedback. You might hear "we went another direction," but that tells you nothing actionable about your performance, communication style, or technical gaps.

### 3. You Can't See the Full Picture
During an interview, you're focused on speaking and thinking. You can't objectively evaluate your tone, pace, hesitations, or how well you actually answered their questions compared to what they were looking for.

### 4. Every Interview Matters
Each interview is a chance to improve. Without real data, you repeat the same mistakes across multiple interviews, reducing your chances of landing an offer.

---

## How Aftly Works

### Step 1: Record Your Interview
Let Aftly listen to both sides of your job interview—your answers and the interviewer's questions. We capture the full conversation.

### Step 2: AI Analysis
Our AI analyzes your performance across multiple dimensions: clarity, confidence, technical accuracy, interview question fit, and communication style.

### Step 3: Get Your Score
Receive a detailed breakdown showing exactly how you performed. See what you did well, what you missed, and specific areas to improve.

### Step 4: Improve & Succeed
Use these insights in your next interview. Track your progress over time and watch your interview performance improve.
